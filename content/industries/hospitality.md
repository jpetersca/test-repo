## 1. INDUSTRY CONTEXT / THE STAKES

**In hospitality, operational friction is immediately visible to guests‚Äîand directly impacts your brand reputation and revenue.**

Whether you're managing luxury hotels, franchise restaurant locations, or travel services, your brand promise depends on consistent execution across dozens or hundreds of properties. A broken HVAC system in a hotel room. A delay in kitchen equipment repair. An IT issue that prevents check-in. A facility problem that impacts the guest experience. General managers spend hours coordinating responses to operational issues rather than focusing on guest satisfaction and team development.

The competitive stakes are high. Guests notice when operations are seamless‚Äîand they definitely notice when they're not. Properties with faster issue resolution see higher satisfaction scores, better online reviews, and stronger repeat business.
